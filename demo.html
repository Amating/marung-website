Prototype Layer Only

This prototype demonstrates the customer journey and inclusion logic. Production architecture requires: AWS-hosted backend, secure APIs, KYC/FICA integration, payment partner integration, audit logging, encryption at rest and in transit, real-time monitoring, fraud detection, and full regulatory controls under the NPS Act 78/1998.

Target Cloud Environment

AWS Africa (Cape Town) Region — af-south-1 · POPIA-aligned hosting · Encrypted storage (AES-256) · Secure API gateway · Real-time monitoring and alerting · Comprehensive audit trails · Disaster recovery architecture · Financial services-grade security posture · Continuous compliance controls.

Regulated Partner Dependencies

Payment processing, virtual card issuing, BIN sponsorship, KYC/FICA onboarding and settlement to be enabled through licensed and regulated infrastructure partners. Marung is not a bank and does not process payments directly. SADC corridor transfers subject to regulated partner pricing and cross-border compliance frameworks.

Who We Are

A South African technology company building with a long-term view of inclusion, access and economic relevance.

Marung Sebaka Technologies is a South African company building with a long-term view of inclusion, access and economic relevance. Our work sits at the intersection of technology and everyday life, with a focus on sectors where better access can create meaningful change.

Our public presence is intentionally simple. We speak clearly about purpose, direction and impact, while deeper operational detail is shared privately in the appropriate professional context.

Marung is a technology company operating in financial services, not a bank. We leverage regulated financial infrastructure partners to deliver secure, accessible and affordable financial services to individuals, families and businesses across South Africa and the broader African continent.

Our focus is not on becoming a bank. Our focus is on innovation, customer experience, financial inclusion and building the next generation of digital financial access for everyone.

"Technology should not leave people behind."

— Madichaba Milosevic, Founder

We envision a future where every person, regardless of income, geography or circumstance, can participate fully in the digital economy through accessible technology designed for inclusion.

Why Marung?

The future of financial services will not be defined by who owns the bank. It will be defined by who creates the best customer experience.

Marung is building the intelligent technology layer that connects people, merchants, financial institutions and payment infrastructure across Africa.

By leveraging regulated partners rather than becoming a bank ourselves, we can innovate faster, reduce costs, expand accessibility and focus entirely on the needs of our users.

Our mission is simple:

  • →Make digital financial services accessible to everyone.
  • →Reduce barriers to participation in the economy.
  • →Enable financial inclusion at scale.
  • →Create a platform that evolves as technology evolves.

Our position

Not a bank

Marung does not hold deposits, lend money or seek a banking licence. We own the customer experience.

Infrastructure partners provide the rails

Regulated financial infrastructure partners handle compliance, payment processing and settlement.

Asset-light by design

This model allows us to scale rapidly, stay agile and focus entirely on solving customer problems.

The technology layer

Marung is the future-facing technology layer that connects Africa's financial ecosystem.

Inclusion by Design

For Marung Sebaka Technologies, inclusion is not a slogan. It is a design principle that shapes how we think about people, services and long-term socio-economic change.

We build in sectors where access, dignity and participation matter most. Our approach is grounded in the realities of people and communities that are too often underserved by traditional systems — and we design around that, not away from it.

  • Principle one
    Build with context, not assumptions.
  • Principle two
    Design for participation, not exclusion.
  • Principle three
    Prioritise dignity, trust and long-term relevance.

Building Through Partnerships

Marung believes meaningful innovation happens through collaboration.

We work alongside established legal, regulatory, infrastructure and technology partners to ensure our solutions are secure, scalable and aligned with industry standards.

Our partnership approach allows us to focus on innovation, accessibility and customer experience while leveraging trusted specialists for critical infrastructure and compliance functions.

This model is intentional. Rather than attempting to replicate what established institutions have built over decades, we build on top of it — adding the agility, user experience and inclusivity layer that traditional systems have struggled to provide.

Partnership principles

Regulated infrastructure

Payment processing, compliance and settlement handled by licensed financial partners.

Legal and regulatory alignment

We engage specialist legal counsel and operate within South Africa's financial services regulatory framework.

Technology collaboration

We partner with cloud and technology providers who share our commitment to reliability and scale.

Strategic co-creation

Key partners are engaged as collaborators, not just vendors. Their expertise shapes what we build.
